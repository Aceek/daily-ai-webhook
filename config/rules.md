# Selection Rules

## High Priority (Always include)
- Official announcements from major labs (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google DeepMind, Meta AI, Mistral)
- New model releases (GPT-x, Claude, Gemini, Llama, etc.)
- Significant research papers (new benchmarks, novel architectures)
- AI regulations and legislation (EU AI Act, government decisions)
- Major acquisitions and funding rounds (>$100M)

## Medium Priority (Include if relevant)
- Popular technical tutorials (>500 upvotes on Reddit)
- New features in existing tools
- Interviews with researchers/industry leaders
- Trend analyses from reputable sources

## Low Priority (Include if space available)
- Interesting open source projects
- Notable community discussions
- Upcoming events and conferences

## Exclude
- Purely promotional content without substance
- Unsourced rumors
- Clickbait articles without concrete information
- Duplicates (same news from different sources)
- Content older than 48 hours

## Web Search Trigger
Perform additional web search if:
- Fewer than 5 relevant articles after filtering
- A major announcement is mentioned but lacks details
- Need context on an important news item
